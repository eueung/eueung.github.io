Biography

Eueung Mulyana is a faculty member of the School of Electrical Engineering and Informatics (SEEI) at Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB). His research interests include advanced networking technology and modern digital infrastructure/services e.g. Cloud and Mobile Computing, Internet of Things, Software Defined Networks. He also initiated the Telecom Creative group at the Telematics Lab, actively mentors and involves in some local startups.
